# Safe Operating Rules

**Last Updated:** [Date]

## General Requirements

All items transported through this Platform must comply with the following safety rules and Singapore regulations.

## Item Size and Weight Restrictions

- **Maximum Weight**: Items must be **less than 1 kilogram (1kg)**
- **Size**: Items must be small enough to be carried comfortably by a commuter on public transport
- **Packaging**: Items must be properly sealed and non-leaking

## Prohibited Items

The following items are **STRICTLY PROHIBITED** and may not be sent through this Platform:

### 1. Tobacco Products
- Cigarettes
- Vapes and e-cigarettes
- Tobacco products of any kind
- Smoking-related paraphernalia

### 2. Alcohol
- Alcoholic beverages of any kind
- Alcohol-based products

### 3. Medical and Pharmaceutical Items
- Prescription drugs
- Over-the-counter medicines
- Supplements and vitamins
- Medical devices
- Any health-related products requiring professional oversight

### 4. Valuables and Currency
- Cash
- Jewellery
- Precious metals or stones
- Valuable collectibles
- Gift cards or vouchers with significant value
- Any items of high monetary value

### 5. Perishable Food Items
- Food that spoils or requires refrigeration
- Fresh produce that may deteriorate
- Any food items with expiration dates

### 6. Dangerous Items
- Weapons of any kind
- Sharp objects (knives, scissors, tools)
- Flammable materials
- Chemicals or hazardous substances
- Liquids that may leak or spill
- Explosive materials
- Any items that pose a safety risk

### 7. Illegal Items
- Any items prohibited by Singapore law
- Controlled substances
- Counterfeit goods
- Pirated or illegal content
- Stolen property

## Singapore MRT and Public Transport Rules

All items must comply with Singapore MRT and public transport regulations:

- Items must be **safe** and pose no risk to other passengers
- Items must be **small** and not obstruct passageways
- Items must be **sealed** and properly packaged
- Items must be **non-leaking** and not create mess or odors
- Items must not violate any public transport policies

## Item Inspection

- The Platform does NOT inspect items before or during transport
- Senders are solely responsible for ensuring items comply with these rules
- Commuters may inspect items before accepting them
- Commuters have the right to refuse any item at any time

## Consequences of Violation

Sending prohibited items may result in:
- Immediate account suspension or termination
- Legal action if items violate Singapore law
- Reporting to relevant authorities
- Permanent ban from the Platform

## Reporting Violations

If you encounter a prohibited item, please report it immediately through the in-app reporting function.

---

**By using this Platform, you agree to comply with all Safe Operating Rules. Violations may result in immediate account termination and legal consequences.**
