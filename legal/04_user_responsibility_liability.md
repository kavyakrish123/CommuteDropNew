# User Responsibility and Liability

**Last Updated:** [Date]

## Sender Responsibilities

As a **Sender**, you are fully responsible for:

### 1. Item Legality and Compliance
- Ensuring the item is **legal** under Singapore law
- Ensuring the item complies with all **Safe Operating Rules**
- Verifying the item is **not prohibited** by Platform rules
- Confirming the item does not violate any regulations or restrictions

### 2. Proper Packaging
- Packaging items securely and safely
- Ensuring items are properly sealed and non-leaking
- Using appropriate packaging materials
- Labeling items clearly if necessary

### 3. Accurate Information
- Providing accurate item descriptions
- Providing correct pickup and delivery locations
- Communicating clearly with commuters
- Disclosing any relevant information about the item

### 4. Compensation Obligation
- **You must compensate the commuter** if any issue arises from your item, including:
  - Damage to the commuter's property
  - Legal issues arising from the item
  - Any costs or expenses incurred by the commuter
  - Any penalties or fines related to the item

### 5. Full Legal Responsibility
- You bear **full legal responsibility** for the item and its contents
- You are responsible for ensuring the item does not violate any laws
- You will be held accountable for any illegal items sent through the Platform

## Commuter Responsibilities

As a **Commuter**, you are responsible for:

### 1. Voluntary Acceptance
- You accept items **voluntarily** and at your own discretion
- You have the right to **decline any item** at any time, including:
  - Before accepting the item
  - Upon inspection of the item
  - If you have concerns about the item's legality or safety
  - For any reason whatsoever

### 2. Item Inspection
- Inspecting items before accepting them (if you choose to do so)
- Verifying items comply with Platform rules
- Refusing items that violate rules or appear suspicious

### 3. Safe Transport
- Transporting items safely and securely
- Following all Singapore public transport regulations
- Ensuring items do not pose risks to yourself or others

### 4. Delivery Completion
- Delivering items to the specified location (if you accept the item)
- Communicating with senders about delivery status
- Handling items with reasonable care

### 5. No Guarantee Obligation
- You are **NOT obligated** to guarantee delivery
- You are **NOT obligated** to meet specific delivery times
- You are **NOT obligated** to accept any item

## Mutual Responsibilities

Both Senders and Commuters must:

### 1. Compliance with Laws
- Comply with all applicable Singapore laws and regulations
- Respect public transport rules and policies
- Follow all Platform rules and guidelines

### 2. Direct Communication
- Communicate directly with each other
- Resolve disputes between themselves
- Not involve the Platform in user-to-user conflicts

### 3. Honest Conduct
- Provide accurate information
- Act in good faith
- Respect other users' rights and safety

## Liability Allocation

- **Senders** are fully liable for their items and any consequences arising from them
- **Commuters** are responsible for their own actions but have no obligation to accept or deliver items
- **The Platform** has no liability for items, transactions, or user conduct
- **Users** must resolve all issues directly between themselves

## Acknowledgment

By using this Platform, you acknowledge that:
- You understand and accept your responsibilities as outlined above
- You will fulfill your obligations as a user
- You will not hold the Platform responsible for issues arising from your use
- You will resolve disputes directly with other users

**These responsibilities are fundamental to using this Platform. Failure to comply may result in account suspension or termination.**
