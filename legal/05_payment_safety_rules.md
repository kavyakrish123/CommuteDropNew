# Payment Safety Rules

**Last Updated:** [Date]

## Payment Method

**ONLY direct user-to-user PayNow transfers are allowed** on this Platform.

## No Platform Fees

The Platform:
- Does NOT charge any fees
- Does NOT take any commission
- Does NOT provide escrow services
- Does NOT handle payments in any way
- Does NOT process transactions

## Payment Nature

### Voluntary Appreciation
- Payment is **voluntary** "thank-you appreciation" from sender to commuter
- Payment is **NOT** a service fee
- Payment is **NOT** a binding commercial transaction
- Payment is **NOT** guaranteed or required

### No Commercial Transaction
- This Platform does NOT facilitate commercial transactions
- Exchanges are voluntary community interactions
- No binding service agreements exist between users
- No commercial relationship is created through the Platform

## User-to-User Payment

### Direct Transfer Only
- All payments must be made **directly** between users
- Payments are made using **PayNow** (Singapore's instant payment system)
- The Platform does NOT facilitate, process, or handle payments
- Users are responsible for their own payment arrangements

### Payment Timing
- Payment timing is determined **entirely by users**
- The Platform does NOT require payment before or after delivery
- Users may agree on payment terms directly
- No payment is guaranteed or enforced by the Platform

## Payment Risks

### User Responsibility
- Users are **solely responsible** for payment arrangements
- The Platform is **NOT responsible** for:
  - Payment disputes
  - Non-payment issues
  - Payment fraud or scams
  - Payment delays or failures
  - Any payment-related problems

### No Protection
- The Platform provides **NO payment protection**
- The Platform provides **NO escrow services**
- The Platform provides **NO dispute resolution** for payments
- The Platform provides **NO refund mechanisms**

## Payment Disputes

- All payment disputes must be resolved **directly between users**
- The Platform does NOT mediate payment issues
- The Platform does NOT provide customer service for payments
- Users are responsible for resolving their own payment matters

## Prohibited Payment Practices

The following are **NOT allowed**:
- Requesting payment through the Platform
- Using Platform features to enforce payment
- Reporting non-payment to the Platform (unless it involves fraud or illegal activity)
- Expecting Platform intervention in payment matters

## Acknowledgment

By using this Platform, you acknowledge that:
- Payments are voluntary and not guaranteed
- The Platform does not handle or process payments
- You are responsible for your own payment arrangements
- You will not hold the Platform responsible for payment issues
- You will resolve payment disputes directly with other users

---

**These payment rules are fundamental to using this Platform. The Platform is a connector only and does not participate in financial transactions.**
