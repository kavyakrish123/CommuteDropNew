# Privacy Policy (Simple Version)

**Last Updated:** [Date]

## Introduction

This Privacy Policy explains how CommuteDrop ("the Platform", "we", "us") collects, uses, and protects your personal information. This is a simple, straightforward policy for our beta community platform.

## Information We Collect

We collect only **minimal data** necessary for the Platform to function:

### Required Information
- **Phone Number**: For account creation and user verification
- **Profile Name**: A display name for your profile (can be a nickname)
- **Delivery Locations**: Pickup and delivery addresses for matching commuters and senders

### Optional Information
- Profile photo (if you choose to upload one)
- Additional profile information you choose to provide

## How We Use Your Information

Your information is used **only** for:

### 1. Platform Functionality
- **Matching**: Connecting commuters with senders based on routes and locations
- **Communication**: Enabling in-app messaging between users
- **Account Management**: Maintaining your user account and preferences

### 2. Platform Operations
- User authentication and account security
- Platform administration and support
- Compliance with legal requirements

## What We Do NOT Do

We **do NOT**:
- Share your information with third parties
- Sell your data to advertisers or marketers
- Use your information for commercial purposes outside the Platform
- Share your phone number or personal details with other users (except your profile name)
- Track your location beyond what is necessary for matching

## Data Sharing

### No Third-Party Sharing
- Your information is **NOT shared** with third parties
- Your information is **NOT sold** or rented
- Your information is **NOT used** for advertising or marketing by external parties

### Limited Internal Use
- Platform administrators may access your information only for:
  - Platform support and troubleshooting
  - Safety and security purposes
  - Compliance with legal obligations

## Data Security

We take reasonable measures to protect your information:
- Secure data storage
- Access controls for Platform administrators
- Regular security reviews

However, please note that **no system is 100% secure**, and we cannot guarantee absolute security of your data.

## User Rights

### Account Deletion
You have the right to **request account deletion at any time**:
- Contact us through the in-app support function
- Request account deletion
- We will delete your account and associated data within [specified timeframe]

### Data Access
You can request access to your personal data at any time through the in-app support function.

### Data Correction
You can update your profile information directly in the app at any time.

## Data Retention

- We retain your data **only while your account is active**
- Upon account deletion, we will delete your personal information
- Some data may be retained for legal compliance purposes if required by Singapore law

## Location Data

- Location data is used **only** for matching commuters and senders
- Location data is **not** stored beyond what is necessary for active delivery requests
- You can control location sharing through your device settings

## Children's Privacy

This Platform is **not intended for users under 18 years of age**. We do not knowingly collect information from minors.

## Changes to Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify users of significant changes through the app or via email.

## Contact

For questions about this Privacy Policy or to request account deletion, please contact us through the in-app support function.

## Consent

By using this Platform, you consent to:
- The collection and use of your information as described in this Privacy Policy
- The processing of your data for Platform functionality
- The terms outlined in this policy
