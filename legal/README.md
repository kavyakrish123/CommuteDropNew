# Legal and Safety Documentation

This directory contains all legal and safety documentation for the CommuteDrop beta platform.

## File Index

### 1. Beta Disclaimer (`01_beta_disclaimer.md`)
- States the app is in early testing phase
- Clarifies platform is not a formal logistics service
- Explains voluntary nature and user responsibility
- Details platform rights and no-guarantee policy

### 2. Safe Operating Rules (`02_safe_operating_rules.md`)
- Singapore-specific safety rules
- Item size and weight restrictions (<1kg)
- Comprehensive list of prohibited items
- MRT and public transport compliance requirements

### 3. Platform Liability Clause (`03_platform_liability_clause.md`)
- Platform assumes no responsibility for items
- No dispute resolution by platform
- No content inspection or storage
- Platform is only a digital connector

### 4. User Responsibility and Liability (`04_user_responsibility_liability.md`)
- Sender responsibilities (legality, packaging, compensation)
- Commuter responsibilities (voluntary acceptance, right to decline)
- Mutual responsibilities and compliance requirements
- Liability allocation between users

### 5. Payment Safety Rules (`05_payment_safety_rules.md`)
- Only direct PayNow transfers allowed
- No platform fees or commissions
- Payment is voluntary appreciation, not service fee
- No commercial transaction guarantees

### 6. Terms of Use - Beta Version (`06_terms_of_use_beta.md`)
- Platform nature and experimental status
- No commercial guarantees
- Account management and termination rights
- Compliance with Singapore law requirements

### 7. In-App Warning Popups (`07_in_app_warnings.md`)
- Four key warning messages for display
- Implementation notes and display frequency
- User acknowledgment requirements
- Design and technical considerations

### 8. Reporting and Safety (`08_reporting_safety.md`)
- Reporting categories and process
- Automatic user blocking and admin notification
- False report consequences
- Emergency situation handling

### 9. Privacy Policy - Simple Version (`09_privacy_policy_simple.md`)
- Minimal data collection (phone, name, locations)
- No third-party sharing
- User rights (deletion, access, correction)
- Data security and retention policies

## Usage in Mobile App

These files are designed to be:
- Embedded directly into the mobile app (Ionic/React/Capacitor)
- Displayed as full pages or modals
- Referenced in user flows and onboarding
- Used for in-app legal documentation

## Implementation Recommendations

1. **Onboarding Flow**: Display Beta Disclaimer and key warnings
2. **Settings/Help**: Link to all legal documents
3. **Request Creation**: Show Safe Operating Rules and warnings
4. **User Profile**: Include Terms of Use acceptance
5. **Payment Flow**: Display Payment Safety Rules
6. **Reporting Feature**: Reference Reporting and Safety document

## Legal Review

⚠️ **Important**: These documents should be reviewed by a qualified Singapore legal professional before production use. They are designed for a beta community platform and may need adjustments based on:
- Specific Singapore regulations
- Platform evolution
- User feedback
- Legal counsel recommendations
